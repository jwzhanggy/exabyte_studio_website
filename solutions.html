Professional Workflows

End-to-end workflows designed for real-world production scenarios.

1

3D Reconstruction Workflow

Transform multi-view photographs into detailed 3D models using VGGT, Gaussian Splatting, or COLMAP. Perfect for architectural documentation, object digitization, and scene reconstruction.
1
Image Capture
Capture multiple photos of the subject from different angles. Support for DSLR, drone, or smartphone cameras.
2
Feature Extraction
Automatic feature detection and matching across images. SIFT, ORB, or transformer-based features.
3
Reconstruction
Choose algorithm: VGGT for transformer-based, Gaussian Splatting for real-time, or COLMAP for classical SfM.
4
Refinement
Optimize geometry, fill holes, smooth surfaces, and enhance texture quality with built-in tools.
5
Export
Export to PLY, OBJ, glTF, or Gaussian Splat formats. VR-ready output for immersive viewing.
2

Video Production Workflow

Professional multi-track video editing with real-time effects, color grading, and GPU-accelerated encoding. Support for 4K/8K resolution and professional codecs like ProRes and H.265.
1
Import Media
Import video, audio, and images. Support for all major formats including RAW, ProRes, and H.265.
2
Timeline Editing
Multi-track timeline with non-destructive editing. Cut, trim, transitions, and keyframe animations.
3
Effects & Grading
Apply real-time effects, professional color grading, and AI-powered enhancements.
4
Audio Mixing
Multi-channel audio mixing with spatial audio support. HRTF binaural processing for immersive sound.
5
Render
GPU-accelerated encoding to ProRes, H.265, or other formats. Hardware encoding support for faster exports.
3

Spatial Audio Workflow

Create immersive 3D audio experiences with 3rd-order Ambisonics, HRTF processing, and multi-channel mixing. Perfect for VR experiences, 360¬∞ videos, and spatial installations.
1
Audio Recording
Record with spatial microphone arrays or import existing audio. Support for up to 20 channels.
2
Spatial Positioning
Position audio sources in 3D space. Visualize soundfield in real-time with spatial editor.
3
Ambisonics Encoding
Encode to 3rd-order Ambisonics format. Support for higher orders and custom speaker configurations.
4
HRTF Processing
Apply binaural processing for headphone playback. Realistic 3D audio perception with head tracking support.
5
Export
Export to various formats: binaural stereo, multi-channel, or Ambisonics. VR-ready audio for platforms.
4

AI-Powered Generation Workflow

Leverage AI models like ExabyteImage, ExabyteVideo, and ExabyteWorld to generate high-quality content from text prompts or transform panoramas into 3D environments.
1
Prompt Engineering
Craft detailed text prompts describing the desired output. Use style keywords and compositional guidance.
2
Model Selection
Choose model: ExabyteImage for images, ExabyteVideo for videos, or ExabyteWorld for 3D scenes.
3
Generation
AI generates content based on your prompt. Adjust parameters like resolution, duration, or style.
4
Refinement
Iteratively refine results with prompt adjustments or use generated content as starting point for editing.
5
Integration
Integrate AI-generated assets into your projects. Combine with manual edits for hybrid workflows.
